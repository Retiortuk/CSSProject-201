About Us

Welcome to Kopiku.id, where every cup tells a story of passion, quality, and community. Nestled in the heart of Pondokgede, West Java, our coffee shop is dedicated to bringing you the finest freshly roasted coffee, sourced from the best local and international beans.

At Kopiku.id, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it’s an experience. Our skilled baristas craft each cup with precision and care, ensuring that every sip is a moment of pure delight. Whether you’re a coffee connoisseur or just starting your journey, our diverse menu offers something for everyone, from rich espressos to creamy lattes and everything in between.

Join us at Kopiku.id and discover the true essence of coffee. We look forward to serving you and becoming a part of your daily routine.
